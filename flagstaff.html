About Flagstaff

  1. Flagstaff is located near the southwestern edge of the Colorado Plateau and along the western side of the largest contiguous Ponderosa Pine forest in the continental United States.
  2. The city is a hub for astronomical research, home to the Lowell Observatory.
  3. Flagstaff has a much cooler climate compared to other cities in Arizona due to its high elevation.
  4. It is a gateway to the San Francisco Peaks, the highest mountain range in the state of Arizona.
  5. The famous Route 66 runs directly through Flagstaff.
