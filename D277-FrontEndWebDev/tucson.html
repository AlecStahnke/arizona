Tucson

  1. Tucson is surrounded by five minor mountain ranges: the Santa Catalina Mountains, the Tortolita Mountains, the Santa Rita Mountains, the Rincon Mountains, and the Tucson Mountains.
  2. The city is home to the University of Arizona.
  3. Tucson has a desert climate but is cooler and wetter than Phoenix, thanks to its higher elevation.
  4. It is known for the Tucson Gem & Mineral Show, the largest gem and mineral show in the world.
  5. Tucson is one of the oldest continuously inhabited places in North America.
